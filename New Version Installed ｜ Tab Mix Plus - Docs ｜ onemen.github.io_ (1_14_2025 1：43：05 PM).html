Hey there,

It's hard to believe, but it's been 20 years since I launched Tab Mix Plus back in the early days of Firefox! My passion has always been to make your browsing experience smoother and more efficient by enhancing Firefox's tab abilities.

But keeping Tab Mix Plus top-notch and evolving with web standards takes continuous effort. New features take time to develop, and Firefox changes require my attention all the time. That's where you come in, my friend.

A small, monthly donation - think the price of your morning coffee - could make a world of difference.

Sincerely, thank you for your unwavering support. Together, let's keep Firefox a more awesome place to browse the internet!

Always up-to-date

I am maintaining Tab Mix Plus since Firefox 1.0.
I continue to improve Tab Mix Plus by fixing bugs and compatibility problems with other extensions and themes, as well as other Firefox's based browsers like Waterfox, Floorp and LibreWolf.

Visit our Change log pages for Complete List of Changes and Bugs Fixed.
Visit Tab Mix Plus Help.

Bug report

To report a bug first go to our discussions section of our repository. You can also look at our Troubleshooting page. Still have a problem, open an issue at our Github repository

Development Channel

The Development Channel lets you test an experimental new version of this add-on before it's released to the general public.

Contribute code

Anyone who wants to contribute code or assist in any other way can contact me directly via tabmix.onemen@gmail.com, or by opening pull request or issue at our Code repository or our Docs repository
